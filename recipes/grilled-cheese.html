Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Description

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again. In this recipe, you'll fine a detailed, but simple set of instructions on how to make a delicious grilled cheese sandwich!

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Sliced cheese
  • Butter or Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
