Air Fried Hot Dogs Recipe

Description

These air fryer hot dogs in toasted buns turn out perfectly crisp in minutes with the help of your air fryer. Top with ketchup, mustard, relish, chili, or condiments of choice. There's no need to fire up the grill - these air fryer hot dogs are crispy, juicy, and come together in mere minutes.

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog Buns
  • Hot Dogs
  • Condiments of your choice, typically ketchup or mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degress F
  2. Place buns in air fryer basket, cook for about 2 minutes, or until crisp
  3. Place hot dogs in air fryer basket, cook for about 3 minutes. Serve hot dogs in toasted buns.
